Working Nigerian Parents

Parents work so that they can earn enough money to provide well for their children.

In the past,Nigerian women were less likely to work away from their homes and children.

Instead many worked as traders from their homes.

There are advantages and disadvantages to working parents in a family.

Advantages of working Nigerian parents

  1. The family will have more income.
  2. Fathers will become more involved in looking after their children if their wives also work.
  3. Parents who stay at home with their children all day may get angry or impatient with children.There fore, working parents may have a better relationship with their children.

Disadvantages of working Nigerian parents

  1. Baby sitters employed to look after the children may influence them negatively.
  2. Children may not have adequate attention from their parents.
