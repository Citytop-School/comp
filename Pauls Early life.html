Paul's early life

Paul was a great apostle of God but before then his name was Saul.

He was born in Tarsus,a city in Cilicia

Paul was a Jew but he was born in a Roman territory so he was both Hebrew and a Roman citizen.

He was the son of a Pharisee later he became a lawyer in Jewish laws and studied under the famous teacher Gamaliel.

Gamaliel was a Pharisee,a great teacher of all Jewish laws and was well respected by many.

Paul's religion was Judaism [follower of the Jewish laws].

Before his encounters with God Saul persecuted Christians,arrested many and killed some.

He entered into every house and arrested men and women in the Early Church.

He saw Jesus as a deceiver and Christianity as a threat to Judaism.

This made him hate Christians and he was determined to destroy christianity altogether.
