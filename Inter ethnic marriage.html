Inter ethnic marriage

An ethnic group is a group of people from the same region of a country.

There are over 250 ethnic groups as themselves it is called an intra-ethnic marriage.

While inter-ethnic marriage is a marriage between different ethnic groups.

Advantages of inter ethnic marriage

  1. It brings different backgrounds and cultures together.
  2. It can lead to ethnic peace in Nigeria
  3. People will love and understand each other

Disadvantages of inter ethnic marriage

  1. This may lead to conflict between husband and wife.
  2. Family members an friends may not be supportive of the marriage.
  3. There may be distance between the families of the married couples.
