Body system

Body system can be defined as groups of organs and tissues that work together to perform important jobs in the body.

The human body

The human body is made up of structure. The human body is made up of many different parts which are;
  1. Internal parts
  2. External parts

We can see the external part of the body but we can't usually see the internal part of the body without specialized equipment.

Examples of internal parts are;

  1. Brain
  2. Stomach
  3. Liver
  4. Kidney
  5. Intestines

Examples of external parts are;

  1. Skin

The different parts of our bodies each have their own special jobs,but they also work closely with other parts in order to functions.
