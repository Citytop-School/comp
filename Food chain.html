Food Chain

Food Chain refers to the order of events in an ecosystem, where one living organism eats another organism and later that organism is consumed by another large organism.

The flow of nutrient and energy from one organism to another at different tropic levels from a food chain.

Tropic level refers to the sequential stages in a food chain,starting with producers at the bottom followed by the primary,secondary and tertiary consumers.

Every level in a food chain is known as a tropic level.
