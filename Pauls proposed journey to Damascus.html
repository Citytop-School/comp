Paul's proposed journey to Damascus

The Jewish leaders did not believe what the Christians taught and called the teachings blasphemy.

Blasphemy is a teaching against God.

However,more people believed in Jesus and this made the Jewish leaders afraid.

Paul was not happy that christianity was fast growing and the miracles that were happening.

So he sent a letter to the synagogue (Jewish place of worship) in Damascus and informed them that he was coming to arrest people who mentioned the name of Jesus and will be sent to jails in Jerusalem.
