God creation

  1. On the first day God created light
  2. On the second day he created the sky
  3. On the third he created the land and seas.He also created plants and trees
  4. On the fourth day he created the moon,stars and the sun
  5. On the fifth day he created sea animals and birds
  6. On the sixteen day he created land animals and humans

The story of creation

In the beginning everything and everywhere was empty and in darkness

The spirit of God was moving over the world.

Then God created the earth and everything in it.

He created it with the words of his mouth. He created all in six days and rested on the seventh

Moral lesson

  1. the story reminds us of Gods existence
  2. it teaches us about Gods power
  3. It tells us about Gods presence everywhere
  4. we learn how he is immortal
