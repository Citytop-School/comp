Types of body system

We have different types of system in our body here are some examples

  1. Nervous System
  2. Circulatory system
  3. Respiratory system
  4. Digestive system
  5. Excretory system

The nervous system

The nervous system controls all other organ system.

It comprises of the following;

  1. The Brain
  2. Spinal Cord
  3. Sensory Organ
  4. Nerves

Nervous system

The circulatory system

The heart and the blood vessel make up the circulatory system.

The heart acts like a pump to circulate blood around the body.

Circulatory system

The respiratory system

This is the means of exchanging oxygen and carbon dioxide that meets the needs of the body.

The respiratory system comprises of the lungs and windpipe.

Lungs and windpipe allows us breath out carbon dioxide and breath in oxygen.

The windpipe is attached to your nose and mouth.

Respiratory system

The digestive system

The digestive system helps in breaking down of food to form substances that can be transported around the body in the blood.

Digestive system

The major organs of the digestive system are;

  1. Stomach
  2. Intestine
  3. Liver

The excretory system

This allows the body to get rid of the wastes.

The kidneys filter waste from blood and then excreted in urine.

The lungs and skin are also involved in excreting waste.
