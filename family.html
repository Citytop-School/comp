Family

A family is a group of people united by blood,marriage,adoption

Families are meant to provide their members with protection,security,companionship etc.The family is a basic unit of the society

The family consists of two major types which are:

  1. Nuclear family
  2. Extended family

Nuclear family is a small group of people

Extended family is a large group of people which are:

  1. Brother
  2. Sister
  3. Cousin
  4. Nephew
  5. Niece
  6. Aunt
  7. Uncle
  8. Grandfather
  9. Grandmother
  10. In laws
